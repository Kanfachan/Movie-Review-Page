Moview Review: Rise Beyond

Rise Beyond unfolds as an inspiring tale of Ethan Carter, a small-town dreamer determined to become a renowned musician. Battling financial hardship and inner doubts, Ethan sets off on a journey defined by grit and growth. With guidance from his wise mentor, Professor Adams, and the unwavering support of his childhood friend, Lena Mitchell, he navigates obstacles and breakthroughs alike. The film is a stirring reminder that true greatness often waits just beyond the boundaries of fear.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (9.2/10)

Cast Members

  • James Holloway as Ethan
  • Sarah Donovan as Lena Mitchell
  • Michael Thompson as Professor Adams
  • Emily Langford as Evelyn Carter (Ethan's mother)
